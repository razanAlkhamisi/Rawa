Detail of the device:

The tool is a sensor that gathers information from a plant's environment and inputs it into a system created using artificial intelligence methods so that the plant receives the proper care.

Advantages:

Save water

Using sensors to monitor plant health, the time and amount of watering can be calculated. Use these methods when watering to ensure the plant gets the right amount of water and to prevent overwatering, which can cause water waste and plant health problems.

Prolonging the life of the plant

This is done by using sensors to determine the appropriate amounts of water, detecting rot or any abnormal shape of the plant, and alerting you to that.

Crop improvement

Through irrigation management, ensuring effective irrigation practices to meet the water needs of the plant, also monitoring the moisture levels of the soil, and providing irrigation during periods of drought to maintain ideal growing conditions and enhance the productivity and quality of the plants crops.

Send alerts

Alerts will be delivered to you in response when artificial intelligence techniques spot any unusual changes in the plant.
